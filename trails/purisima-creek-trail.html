Purisima Creek Trail

Difficulty Difficult
Length 13.1 km
Elevation 561m

Location

Purisima Creek Redwoods Open Space Preserve

Description

A challenging out-and-back trail through towering redwoods alongside Purisima Creek. Features significant elevation gain with rewarding views of the coast on clear days.

Trail Conditions

Open year-round. Trail is well-maintained. Best hiked in cooler months.
