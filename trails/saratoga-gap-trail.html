Saratoga Gap Trail

Difficulty Moderate
Length 7.9 km
Elevation 313m

Location

Castle Rock State Park

Description

A popular loop trail featuring rock scrambles, ridge walks with rope-assisted sections, and beautiful mountain views. Trail varies from exposed rock terraces to shaded redwood forests. Best done counter-clockwise.

Trail Condition

Open year-round. Trail has rocky sections, some poison oak. Mostly shaded with some sun exposure. $10 parking fee.
