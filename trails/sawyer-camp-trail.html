Sawyer Camp Trail

Difficulty Easy
Length 18.7 km
Elevation 139m

Location

Lower Crystal Springs Reservoir

Description

A scenic trail along Crystal Springs Reservoir with gentle elevation changes. Perfect for families and beginners, offering water views and wildlife spotting opportunities.

Trail Conditions

Open year-round. Paved trail, well-maintained. Popular for walking, running, and cycling.
